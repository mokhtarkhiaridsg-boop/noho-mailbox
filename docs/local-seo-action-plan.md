## 4. Review-generation playbook

**Goal:** 30-50 new Google reviews in 30 days. **Rule:** max 5/day; Google flags velocity spikes.

### Get your review link

1. Owner finds Place ID at https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id
2. Direct review link: `https://g.page/r/{PLACE_ID}/review`
3. Shorten with Bitly or `noho.link/review` so it fits in SMS.

### Email script (existing ~500 customers, batched at 5/day)

```
Subject: Quick favor — would you leave us a Google review?

Hi {first_name},

Mokhtar from NOHO Mailbox here. You've been with us for {months} months now,
and I wanted to ask a small favor.

We're a tiny independent shop competing against the chains, and Google reviews
are how new customers find us. If we've done right by you, would you take 30
seconds to leave a short review?

Direct link: {REVIEW_LINK}

Even one sentence helps a lot. Thank you, seriously.

— Mokhtar
NOHO Mailbox
(818) 506-7744
```

### SMS script (after in-store pickup)

```
Hey {first_name}, Mokhtar at NOHO Mailbox. Thanks for stopping in today.
If we made your day easier, would you drop a quick Google review? {REVIEW_LINK}
— takes 20 seconds. Appreciated.
```

### Post-pickup card (printed, 3x4 in)

```
Loved the experience?
Tell Google. Scan → leave a quick review.
{QR CODE to REVIEW_LINK}
We read every one. — Mokhtar & the NOHO team
```

### Receipt footer

`Thanks! → Review us on Google: nohomailbox.org/review`
(Set up `/review` as a 302 redirect to `g.page/r/{PLACE_ID}/review`.)

### Cadence

- Week 1: 5 emails/day to longest-tenured customers
- Week 2: 5 SMS/day to recent pickups
- Week 3-4: handout cards at counter, receipt footer live
- Reply to every review within 24h (signal to Google + future customers)
