## Engine 2 — Franchise (`/franchise`)

### Required setup before serious outreach

- **Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)** — required by FTC + state-by-state. Hire a franchise attorney. ~$10–$20k legal cost.
- **Brand book** — completed already in NOHO Mailbox materials.
- **Operations manual** — needs to be drafted (40–80 hours of work or hire a franchise consultant).
- **Training program** — 5-day on-site curriculum.

Until FDD is filed, **don&apos;t actively sell franchises** in regulated states (CA, NY, IL, MD, MI, MN, ND, RI, SD, VA, WA, WI). The /franchise page can collect interested-party signups but must show the disclaimer (which it does).

### Outreach (post-FDD)

#### A. Existing CMRA owners (Engine 1 customers who graduate)

If a SaaS customer is on our platform for 6+ months and wants to grow into multi-location, pitch franchise as the next step. They already trust us; we provide brand + platform; they put up capital.

- Miami / Brickell / Coral Gables
- Austin / South Austin
- Dallas suburbs
- Phoenix / Scottsdale
- Denver / Boulder

Run targeted Meta ads to "small business owner" / "franchise owner" demographics in these metros.

### Targets (12 months post-FDD)

- 5 signed franchisees
