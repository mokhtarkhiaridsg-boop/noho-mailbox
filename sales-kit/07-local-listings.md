# Local Listings Optimization Checklist

These are free channels that compound. They take 2–4 hours to set up well and pay for years.

**Order:** Google → Apple → Yelp → Nextdoor → Bing/Bing Places → BBB → Niche directories.

---

## 1. Google Business Profile (GBP) — most important

**Why it matters:** when someone in NoHo searches "same day delivery near me" or "LLC formation North Hollywood", GBP is what shows up before any organic result. Free billboard.

### Setup checklist

- [ ] Claim profile at `business.google.com` (you sign in, I can't do it for you).
- [ ] Verify by postcard / phone / video call (Google requires the owner to do this).
- [ ] Categories — set primary + secondary correctly. **This is the single biggest lever.**
  - **Primary:** `Mailing service` (CMRA category)
  - **Secondary** (max 9):
    1. Delivery service
    2. Notary public
    3. Shipping service
    4. Business management consultant
    5. Mail forwarding service
    6. Print shop (if you do print)
    7. Packaging supply store
    8. Courier service
    9. Marketing agency
- [ ] Service area — explicitly add: North Hollywood, Studio City, Burbank, Sherman Oaks, Toluca Lake, Valley Village, Van Nuys, Van Nuys, Universal City. Do not over-claim — if you say "all of LA" Google deprioritizes you.
- [ ] Hours — exact. Add holiday hours quarterly.
- [ ] Phone — match exactly to your site footer (NAP consistency).
- [ ] Website — point to `https://nohomailbox.org`.
- [ ] Description (750 chars max) — write it once, paste this:

> NOHO Mailbox is a family-run private-mailbox and same-day delivery store at 5062 Lankershim Blvd in North Hollywood. We rent real LA street addresses (not P.O. boxes) with 24/7 mail scanning and package alerts, run $5 same-day deliveries inside North Hollywood, notarize mail forms in-house, and offer complete business launch packages — LLC, EIN, brand, and website — under one roof. Real humans answer the phone Mon–Sat. Walk in or call (818) 506-7744.

- [ ] Photos — upload at least 20 (storefront, interior, mailbox wall, your team, packages, delivery vehicle, the Lankershim cross-street). Caption each. Upload one per week ongoing.
- [ ] Logo + cover photo — use the brand PNG.
- [ ] Products — add at least 8: Basic Box, Business Box, Premium Box, Same-Day Delivery, Notary, Business Launch Bundle, Brand Retainer, Pre-Paid Shipping. Each with a price and short description. Each is a tiny landing page Google indexes.
- [ ] Services — same set, less detailed.
- [ ] Q&A — pre-populate 10 common questions yourself and answer them publicly. Searchers see them. Examples:
  - "Do you deliver to Burbank same-day?"
  - "How is this different from a P.O. box?"
  - "Do you do LLC formation?"
  - "Are you open Saturdays?"
  - "Do you accept packages from FedEx and UPS?"
  - "Can I use this address on my driver's license?"
  - "How much is mail forwarding?"
  - "Do you do notary on Form 1583?"
  - "What's included in your $2,000 business launch?"
  - "What's the same-day delivery cost outside NoHo?"
- [ ] Posts — 1 per week minimum. Google rewards activity. Topic ideas in `09-execute.md`.
- [ ] Reviews — see "Review engine" below.

### Review engine (compounds the most)

- [ ] After every transaction, hand the customer a card with a Google review QR code. Or text: "Thanks for using NOHO Mailbox! If you have 30 sec, a Google review really helps us: [short link]. — NOHO Mailbox · STOP to opt out."
- [ ] Target: 50 reviews in 90 days. Average 4.7+. Reply to **every** review (positive and negative) within 48 hours.
- [ ] Never offer a discount in exchange for a review (FTC violation). You can offer general appreciation ("we'd love your honest feedback").

## 2. Apple Business Connect

iPhone users searching in Apple Maps + Siri see this. About 25% of LA local searches.

- [ ] Sign in at `businessconnect.apple.com`.
- [ ] Same NAP as Google.
- [ ] Add the same photos.
- [ ] Add Showcases (free): "Same-Day Delivery", "Business Launch", "Mailbox Plans".
- [ ] Verify with the same phone / postcard flow.

---

## 3. Yelp

Older crowd, but still drives meaningful traffic for shipping/notary searches.

- [ ] Claim at `biz.yelp.com`.
- [ ] Categories: Mailbox Centers, Notaries, Couriers & Delivery Services, Shipping Centers.
- [ ] Hours, photos, description (rewrite slightly so it's not byte-identical to GBP — Yelp penalizes copy-paste).
- [ ] Yelp upsells "Yelp Ads" hard. **Decline.** It rarely pencils out for small storefronts. Free profile only.
- [ ] Review reply + ask cycle, same as GBP.

---

## 4. Nextdoor — Business Page

Nextdoor is the highest-conversion local channel for small B2B + B2C in LA neighborhoods. People actually post asking for recommendations.

- [ ] Create a Business page (`business.nextdoor.com`). It's free.
- [ ] Set service area to: North Hollywood, Studio City, Toluca Lake, Valley Village, Burbank, Sherman Oaks. Each is a separate "neighborhood" Nextdoor recognizes.
- [ ] Pin a welcome post: "We do same-day delivery, mail, notary, and business setup at 5062 Lankershim. First $5 delivery is on us if you mention this post."
- [ ] Engage in 5 posts per week as the business — when someone asks "where do I notarize a Form 1583?" or "anyone know a same-day courier in NoHo?", be the one who answers fast and friendly. Don't pitch — answer.
- [ ] Run a Nextdoor neighborhood-targeted ad **once** in week 4 ($50 cap, 7-day window) to test cost-per-lead.

---

## 5. Bing Places + Bing Webmaster

Bing has small share, but it's free and Microsoft Copilot indexes it heavily. 30 minutes total.

- [ ] `bingplaces.com` → import from GBP (Bing has a one-click GBP import).
- [ ] Verify.
- [ ] Add Bing Webmaster Tools and submit `nohomailbox.org` sitemap.

---

## 6. Niche directories (one-time data entry)

Google ranks businesses higher when their NAP is consistent across many directories. Do these once:

- [ ] Yellow Pages (yellowpages.com)
- [ ] Angi / Angie's List
- [ ] Better Business Bureau (BBB) — get accredited if it pencils ($500/yr, only worth it if you want the trust badge for B2B)
- [ ] Chamber of Commerce — Greater San Fernando Valley + North Hollywood
- [ ] Yelp for Business
- [ ] Foursquare/Swarm (still indexed)
- [ ] Hotfrog
- [ ] Manta
- [ ] BringBackHumans (CMRA-specific directory if it still exists)

A tool like Whitespark or BrightLocal can submit to 50 at once for $30/mo for 3 months — only worth it if you have time-cost > the $90 outlay.

## 7. Industry-specific directories

- [ ] **CMRA registry** — if you're not already on the USPS PS Form 1583-A approved CMRA list, fix that immediately (legal req for the mailbox business).
- [ ] **NotaryRotary** — list yourself for the notary line.
- [ ] **NotaryCafe**
- [ ] **123Notary**
- [ ] **Etsy / Shopify partner directories** for the business solutions side (apply as a service partner).

## 8. Schema.org structured data on your site

This is a website task — flag for later when you're ready to touch the site:

- Add `LocalBusiness` schema with full NAP + opening hours
- Add `Service` schema for delivery, notary, business solutions
- Add `Product` schema for each mailbox plan
- This is what feeds Google's rich results (price chips, "open now", etc.)

---

## NAP consistency rule

Across every channel, the **Name, Address, Phone** must be **byte-identical**:

- Name: `NOHO Mailbox` (NOT "Noho Mailbox", "NoHo Mailbox", or "NOHO Mailbox Inc.")
- Address: `5062 Lankershim Blvd, North Hollywood, CA 91601`
- Phone: `(818) 506-7744`

Inconsistency drops your rank. Pick the canonical form, paste everywhere.
