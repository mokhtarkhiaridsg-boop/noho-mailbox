# Walk-In Day Playbook — Lankershim Corridor

**Goal:** 30 in-person prospect touches in one focused 4-hour day. Drop a card, leave a counter sticker, get a phone number. Closes happen on follow-up — this day is about creating the future pipeline.

**Date:** Pick a Tuesday or Wednesday between 10am and 1:30pm.
**Total walking time:** ~90 min round trip (you can stretch it). Most stops are 1–6 minutes.
**Pre-day:** print 80 partner one-pagers, 80 referral cards, 80 review-request cards, 200 counter stickers, 80 business cards.
**Backpack:** stickers, one-pagers, cards, your phone (with the prospect-tracker spreadsheet open), water, a smile.

---

## Phone-back script (call after walk-in, ~48 hours later)

> "Hi [Name], it's Mokhtar from NOHO Mailbox — we met Tuesday at [address]. Just checking in to see if you've had a chance to think about [delivery / partner program]. No pressure — wanted to keep the offer fresh. Anything I can answer?"

→ If they're cold: "All good — I'll let you go. We're at 5062 Lankershim if anything changes."
→ If lukewarm: "Want me to drop off another stack of cards / one-pagers?"
→ If warm: "Perfect — let's set up a quick 15-minute call. Tuesday or Thursday at 10am?"

## What success looks like

- **30 visits** in one day = you've covered every walking-distance prospect in one trip.
- **5–10 verbal "yes"** = pipeline created.
- **2–3 follow-up calls scheduled within a week** = active pipeline.
- **1 closed (paid run / signed partner / bundle deposit) within 2 weeks** = ROI proven.

If you hit 1 close per outreach day, you're paying for the day in 1 transaction. Run this every 2 weeks for the next quarter and you'll have a stable book.
