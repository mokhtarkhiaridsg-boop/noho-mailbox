## Compliance + Tracking

**CAN-SPAM**: Every email above has an unsubscribe path (Reply STOP or
"reply to unsubscribe"). The sender address is a real human-monitored
inbox. The physical address (5062 Lankershim Blvd) is in every footer.

**Tracking**:
- Use Resend's open + click tracking (free)
- Use UTMs on every link: ?utm_source=email&utm_medium=nurture&utm_campaign=[seq_letter][step_number]
- Reply rate is the most important metric — these emails are written for
  conversation, not just clicks

**Stop conditions**:
- Reply received → manual takeover, stop sequence
- Purchase / conversion → start the customer onboarding sequence
- "Reply STOP" or unsubscribe → immediately remove from all lists

**Frequency caps**:
- No more than 1 email per day per address
- No more than 3 emails per week per address (across all sequences)
- Honor Quiet Hours: nothing 9pm-8am customer time zone

**Personalization**:
- First name pulled from form
- City pulled from form (use in subject line for higher open rates)
- Business type / use case (when known) — use in body to make it feel
  hand-written
