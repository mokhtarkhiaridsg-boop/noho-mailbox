The path to $5M/mo NET monthly cannot come from a single storefront.

That stack realistically reaches $1M–$3M/mo net within 18–24 months. **$5M net is a 5-year horizon** with all four engines running.

## US CMRA market size

USPS lists ~12,000–15,000 registered CMRAs. The platforms most run on:
- Spreadsheets / paper (40% — BIG opportunity)
- Anytime Mailbox / iPostal1 (~20%)
- Mail Boxes Etc / UPS Store custom (~25%)
- Other (PostalAnnex, regional) (~15%)

**Total addressable market for our SaaS:** ~10,000 mid-tier CMRAs. Realistic 5-year capture: 5–15% = 500–1,500 paying SaaS customers. At $299–$799/mo blended ~$500/mo, that's **$250k–$750k/mo recurring**.

## Building the SaaS infra (technical roadmap)

The current platform is single-tenant (one Turso DB, one domain). To productize as SaaS:

1. **Multi-tenancy** — schema change. Add `tenantId` to every table. ~2 weeks of careful migration work.
2. **Onboarding flow** — `/saas-signup` with payment + tenant provisioning. ~1 week.
3. **Operator dashboard** — track all tenants, billing, usage. ~2 weeks.
4. **Brand kit per tenant** — logo, colors, custom subdomain (e.g. `acme-mailbox.nohomailbox.org`). ~1 week.
5. **Stripe billing** — subscription + metered. ~1 week.

**Total to launch v1 SaaS:** 6–8 weeks, mostly DB and onboarding work. Most existing features already work per-tenant if `tenantId` is wired correctly.
