## What NOT to do

- **Don't paste these templates verbatim.** Algorithms detect copy-paste
  across multiple accounts. Adapt every one.
- **Don't fake the cons.** If you write a review, include real cons.
  Otherwise readers don't trust you and it dies.
- **Don't promise outcomes you can't deliver.** "I made $50k from this
  mailbox" — no. "I use this address for my Etsy shop" — yes.
- **Don't run paid ads on our brand terms.** "NOHO Mailbox" branded
  searches are ours. Run on category terms instead ("LA business
  address," "California LLC formation").
- **Don't use shortened URLs that strip our attribution.** Bit.ly /
  tinyurl preserve UTMs but some social platforms strip them.
