#### Template B — "How do I form an LLC in California?"

> The state filing is $70 (Articles of Organization, Form LLC-1), and you need a real street address — not a P.O. Box. After that:
>
> 1. EIN — free on irs.gov, takes 15 min
> 2. Statement of Information ($20) — within 90 days, then every 2 years
> 3. **$800/year franchise tax** — even if you make $0. Plan for it.
> 4. Operating agreement — required even though you don&apos;t file it
>
> If you&apos;re bootstrapping under $20k revenue, sole-prop + DBA is often cheaper. We do all-in LLC + brand + site + a year of mail at our LA address for $2k flat at NOHO Mailbox if you want one shop, but plenty of solo paths too. — Mokhtar in NoHo
