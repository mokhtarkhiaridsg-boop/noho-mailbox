# Paid Ads Copy Pack

You log in to each platform and paste these. **Don't launch all at once** — start with Google LSA + Meta location-targeted ads. Add channels as the cheaper free channels (GBP, organic) saturate.

**Spend recommendation for first 30 days:**
- Google Local Service Ads: $300/mo cap (only pays per actual lead)
- Meta location-targeted: $200/mo
- Yelp: $0 (decline upsells)
- Nextdoor sponsored post: $50 once

That's $550/mo cap. If you close 1 Business Solutions Bundle at $2,000 from this spend, ROI is ~3.6×. If you don't, kill the spend after 30 days and re-tune.

---

## 1. Google Local Service Ads (LSA)

**Why:** pay-per-lead, not per-click. Google qualifies the lead (phone call or message) before charging you. Ranked above paid Search Ads in mobile.

**Categories to enable:**
- Mailing services
- Notary public
- Courier services

**Service area:** North Hollywood, Studio City, Burbank, Sherman Oaks, Toluca Lake, Valley Village, Universal City, Van Nuys.

**Bid strategy:** "Maximize Leads" with $25 max per lead.

**Hours of operation:** match your storefront hours. Disable after-hours leads (only mail back, don't pay for them).

**Photos:** upload the same 8 you use on GBP — storefront, interior, mailbox wall, team, vehicle.

**Reviews:** LSA pulls from your GBP reviews. Make sure GBP is at 10+ reviews before launching.

## 2. Meta (Facebook + Instagram) Ads

### Campaign A — Same-day delivery, location-targeted

**Objective:** Leads
**Audience:**
- Location: 5-mile radius around 91601
- Age: 25–65
- Detailed targeting: business owners, lawyers, real estate, florists, retail, e-commerce, freelance, dental practice, medical practice
- Behaviors: small business owners

## 3. Yelp

Decline Yelp Ads. They rarely pencil for storefronts of this size. Free profile only. Keep an eye on inbound messages.

**Yelp profile copy** (bio):
> NOHO Mailbox is a family-run private mailbox and same-day delivery store at 5062 Lankershim Blvd. Real LA street addresses (not P.O. boxes), $5 flat same-day delivery in NoHo, walk-in notary, and full business launch packages. Real humans answer the phone Mon–Sat.

## 5. Google Search Ads (deferred)

Don&apos;t run until LSA is dialed. When you do, run these keywords with exact-match:

- `[same day delivery north hollywood]` — bid $4
- `[courier 91601]` — $4
- `[private mailbox north hollywood]` — $3
- `[llc formation north hollywood]` — $5
- `[notary north hollywood]` — $2

Exclude broad match — too expensive in LA.

## 6. TikTok / Instagram organic content (free, owner-only)

Topics that perform for local businesses:

- "$5 deliveries in NoHo — POV behind the counter" (60s vlog)
- "Why the post office rejected this Form 1583" (educational)
- "California LLC: actual cost in 2026" (no clickbait, just honest math)
- "Etsy seller's mistake — using a P.O. Box" (story)
- "Mother's Day overflow with [local florist]" (collaboration)

3 posts/week minimum. Every video ends with "(818) 506-7744" or @ the storefront IG handle.

## 7. Tracking

For each campaign, capture per-channel:

```
| Channel | Spend | Leads | Closes | Closed $ | CPL | ROAS |
```

Review every Friday. Cut anything below ROAS 1.5×.

---

## 8. Don't

- ❌ Don't run all campaigns simultaneously week 1 — you won't know what's working
- ❌ Don't accept Yelp's enhanced-profile upsell ($300+/mo)
- ❌ Don't bid on competitors' brand names ("ClockWork", "California Courier")
- ❌ Don't run national Search Ads — must be geo-targeted to LA
- ❌ Don't use stock photos in ads — Google and Meta both penalize them and reviewers see through them

## 9. Order of operations

- **Week 1:** Launch GBP optimization (free) + LSA (paid). No other paid spend.
- **Week 3:** Add Meta Campaign A (same-day delivery) at $50/day cap.
- **Week 5:** Review LSA + Meta. Kill non-performers. Add Meta Campaign B if A is converting.
- **Week 7:** Layer in Nextdoor sponsored post.
- **Month 3:** Evaluate Google Search Ads only if LSA + Meta are saturated.
