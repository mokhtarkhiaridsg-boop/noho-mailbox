# Partner Referral Program

## The deal in one paragraph

> When a partner sends a customer to NOHO Mailbox and that customer pays, we pay the partner **15% of the first invoice** within 14 days of cleared funds. On any recurring product (mailbox plan, brand-management retainer, recurring delivery route), we pay **15% of every recurring invoice for the first 12 months**.

Simple, generous enough to actually move (LegalZoom affiliate is 5%), nothing complicated to track.

| Pre-paid label / shipping | $0 | Margin already thin |

## How we track referrals

Three ways, partner picks (whichever is easiest for *them*):

1. **Partner code** — partner gives client a 4-letter code (e.g. `ROLAND`, `IMMIGLAW`). Client mentions it on signup or pays online with it. Cleanest. **Recommended.**
2. **"Sent by [name]" check-box** on the signup form (already in the request-a-mailbox flow — confirm it's there, add if not).
3. **Email handoff** — partner emails `partners@nohomailbox.org` with the prospect's name; we tag the lead.

We track in a spreadsheet that audits cleanly:

```
| Partner | Code | Lead | First touch date | Closed date | Product | Paid amount | Commission | Paid out date |
```

## Payout method

- Pay via Square (transfer to partner's connected bank) or check, partner's choice.
- Cut checks **every other Friday** for everything that cleared in the prior 14 days.
- Send a copy of the receipt to the partner with each payment so trust compounds.

## How to bring a partner on (first conversation)

1. Walk in to their office (don't email a partner pitch first; they ignore those).
2. Hand them a one-pager (below).
3. Pitch in 60 seconds:
   > "We pay 15% on every client you send. $300 on a full business setup, $180/mo for a year on a retainer. Our office is three blocks from yours. We don't compete with what you do — we're the next step after you finish. Want to try it with one client this month?"
4. Ask them: *"Who's the next client coming in who already needs an LLC?"*
5. Schedule a 15-min coffee or counter chat in 2 weeks to review.

## How to keep a partner active

- Send the partner the **first commission check within 30 days of pitching** — even if it's $50. Speed of first payout = retention.
- Check in **every 30 days** with a short text: "Three blocks away. Anything I can help with this week?"
- Once a partner has sent 3 referrals, send them something physical — branded mug, gift card to a nearby cafe, etc. ($25 budget, big retention lift).
- If a partner goes silent for 60 days, ask them why directly. Almost always it's "I forgot you existed." Fix that with a counter sticker or a co-branded form.

## Partner agreement (one-page)

Plain English, no JD-required. Save as `/Users/CEO/Claude/noho-mailbox/sales-kit/06a-partner-agreement.md` if you want to formalize. For most partners a handshake + the spreadsheet is enough until volume grows.

Key terms to include in the formal version:
- Commission percentages (above)
- Payout cadence (every other Friday)
- Term: month-to-month, 30-day written notice to terminate
- No-poach: if you let a partner go, residual commissions on closed deals continue for the 12-month term
- Confidentiality on customer info (CCPA compliance)
- Either party can pause the program with 30 days' notice
