# Cold Email Templates

**Anti-spam reality check before you send anything:**

US CAN-SPAM lets you cold-email B2B if you (1) accurately identify the sender, (2) include a real physical address, (3) honor opt-outs within 10 days, (4) don't use deceptive subject lines. CA CCPA + CASL (Canadian customers) are stricter — keep your list to US-only for now.

**Hard rules:**
- Never use the gmail address or a personal address for cold outbound. Use `hello@nohomailbox.org` (now that the domain verifies).
- Always include the storefront address (5062 Lankershim Blvd, North Hollywood, CA 91601) in the footer.
- Always include a one-click unsubscribe ("reply STOP" works for SMS, "reply UNSUBSCRIBE" for email).
- Cap at **40 sends/day from one address** for the first 2 weeks while the new domain warms up. More than that and Resend/Gmail will throttle you and your domain reputation tanks.
- Personalize line 1. Generic mass-blast = burned domain.

## Follow-ups

**Rule of thumb:** 3 touches over 14 days, then mark dead.

### Follow-up 1 (day +3, no reply)

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

> Hi [Name] — quick bump in case my first email got buried. Same offer, still good. — [Your name]

### Follow-up 2 (day +10, no reply)

**Subject:** Last note from us

> [Name] — last note from us. If same-day delivery (or business setup) isn't useful right now, no worries — we'll stop bothering you. If it ever changes, we're at (818) 506-7744 and we won't email again.

## Subject lines that work for local B2B (steal these)

- "$5 flat in NoHo"
- "Court runs — same hour"
- "Mother's Day overflow"
- "[Their first name], one-line question"
- "Saw you're at [their cross street]"
- "Three blocks from us"
- "$300 per referral"

## Subject lines that get you junked

- ❌ "Important update from NOHO Mailbox"
- ❌ "FREE delivery limited time!!"
- ❌ "Re: our previous conversation" (when you haven't had one)
- ❌ ALL CAPS anywhere
- ❌ Emoji in subject

## Sending mechanics

- **Tool:** for batches under 100/day, send from `hello@nohomailbox.org` directly via the Resend API or via Gmail "send as" with the alias. For larger volume use Instantly, Apollo, or Smartlead — they handle warmup and unsubscribe handling.
- **Don't send from `noreply@`** — looks like a no-reply broadcaster, lower reply rate.
- **Track opens minimally.** Heavy tracking pixels also kill deliverability.
- **First send time:** Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday 9:30–11:00 local. Saturdays for restaurant/florist owners only.
