# Paths to $5M Net Monthly — The Honest Plan

You said $5M net / month. That's $60M ARR. Let me walk through what realistically gets there, with honest math and timeline.

**Today:** ~$5–10k/mo gross from a single NoHo storefront with 24 customers. To hit $5M net we need a 500–1000× scale-up. Single-storefront math caps at ~$50k/mo net. So **the path requires multi-engine.**

Here are the 6 engines, ranked by realistic leverage:

## Engine 1 — CMRA Platform SaaS (the biggest leverage)

**What it is:** license the software running NOHO Mailbox to other CMRA operators across the US (12,000+ CMRAs registered with USPS).

**Math:**
- Solo tier $299/mo · Multi-location $799 · Enterprise $1,499+
- Realistic year 3: 1,000 customers × $400 avg = **$400k/mo recurring**, 80% gross margin = $320k/mo net
- Year 5 ceiling: 3,000 customers × $500 = $1.5M/mo recurring, ~$1.2M/mo net

**Time to $1M/mo:** 24–36 months
**Cost to start:** ~$50k of dev work to make multi-tenant
**Hire:** 1 sales rep ($120k/yr), 1 customer success ($90k/yr) by month 12
**Already shipped:** `/for-cmra-operators` landing page · `sales-kit/15-cmra-saas-pitch-pack.md` · all the pitch material

This is the ONLY engine with realistic path to seven figures monthly. **Make it priority 1.**

## Engine 2 — Franchise the Storefront

**What it is:** sell franchise rights to NOHO Mailbox storefronts. Franchisees pay an upfront fee + recurring royalty. They run the location, you keep the brand + platform fee.

**Math:**
- Franchise fee: $50k–$100k upfront
- Royalty: 6% of gross + $500/mo platform fee
- Realistic year 5: 50 franchisees × $30k/yr royalty + $6k/yr platform = $36k/yr per location
- 50 × $36k = **$1.8M/yr from franchise stream** ($150k/mo net)
- Plus the $5M aggregate franchise fees ($2.5–5M one-time)

**Time to first franchisee:** 12 months (FDD legal, brand book, ops manual, training program)
**Cost:** ~$150k for FDD + legal + brand work
**Hire:** Franchise development VP ($150k + commission)

This is medium-term. Need the SaaS engine to provide the operational backbone first.

## Engine 3 — Enterprise Courier Contracts

**What it is:** sell large-volume same-day courier contracts to enterprise accounts (legal firms, hospital systems, financial institutions).

**Math:**
- $20k–$80k/mo per contract
- Realistic year 2: 10 enterprise contracts at $40k avg = **$400k/mo gross**
- Net margin ~25% (you pay drivers, fuel, dispatch) = $100k/mo net
- Year 5 ceiling: 30 contracts × $60k = $1.8M/mo gross, $450k/mo net

**Time to first enterprise contract:** 6–12 months (relationship sales)
**Cost:** ~$100k for senior B2B sales rep
**Hire:** 1 enterprise courier sales rep + dispatcher

This is **traditional B2B sales** — long cycles, big checks. Best paired with the SaaS engine (cross-sell platform to enterprise accounts).
