# Quick Reference — "I Need Help With X"

Map your situation to the right playbook. Skip the full kit; jump
straight to what helps.

---

## "I just woke up and don't know where to start"

Read in this order, total 60 minutes:

1. `_OVERNIGHT-BUILD-SUMMARY.md` (10 min) — what got built
2. `38-first-90-days-roadmap.md` (15 min) — exact day-by-day execution
3. `45-founders-operating-system.md` (10 min) — daily/weekly cadence
4. `22-daily-playbook-30min.md` (10 min) — what 30 min/day looks like
5. `31-inbound-triage-playbook.md` (15 min) — how to handle leads when they arrive

Then start executing. Day 1 actions are in doc 38.

## "I have an inbound lead RIGHT NOW"

Doc 31 (inbound triage). Tier-tag in 60 seconds, response template
in 4 minutes.

- **Tier A** ($2k bundle / SaaS demo / partner): respond within 1 hour
- **Tier B** (mailbox / delivery / affiliate): respond within 4 hours
- **Tier C** (newsletter / general): auto-nurture handles it
- **Tier D** (spam): mark as spam, no reply

## "I want to delegate / hire help"

Doc 36 (hiring roadmap) — when each role becomes urgent.

If pre-revenue ($30k MRR): don&apos;t hire. Use the playbooks.
If $30-$50k MRR: first hire = storefront associate (doc 36 Stage 2).
If $40-$80k MRR: second hire = driver (Stage 3).
If $60-$120k MRR: third hire = customer success / sales (Stage 4).

## "I'm burning out"

Doc 45 (founder&apos;s OS) — burnout section.

Take 3-5 days off. Hire help (doc 36). Cut scope. Don&apos;t push
through.

If you&apos;re consistently working 60+ hours/week for 4+ weeks,
this is the symptom. Address now, not when you crash.

## What's NOT in this kit

If your situation isn&apos;t covered:

- **International expansion (UK / EU / Canada)**: not in scope.
  Refer to international LLC formation services.
- **Niche industries** (cannabis, adult, regulated industries):
  unique compliance requirements; consult industry-specific
  advisors.
- **Capital raises beyond bootstrap**: doc 44 covers basics; for
  Series A+ work with an investment banker.
- **M&A as a buyer**: doc 44 is from seller perspective. Buying
  another company needs a separate playbook.
