# Lane A — Same-Day Delivery: Prospect List

**How to use this list:**
1. Walk in. Don't email first. You're 4 blocks from most of these.
2. Bring 5 partner-program one-pagers + 5 business cards + your phone (open the order form).
3. Pitch in 30 seconds: *"Hi — I run NOHO Mailbox at 5062 Lankershim. We do five-dollar same-day inside NoHo. If you ever need something across the Valley today, we're closer than [their current courier]. Here's a card. First run is free."*
4. Mark every visit in your phone (name, person you talked to, outcome). Follow up in 7 days.

## How to score and prioritize

For each prospect, before walking in:
- **Distance:** ≤ 1 mile = walk now. 1–3 mi = drive this week. >3 mi = email/SMS only.
- **Frequency potential:** does this business send packages/papers daily? If no, deprioritize.
- **Decision-maker access:** can you talk to the owner/manager same day? If gatekeeper-only, mail a one-pager and follow up.

Track in a Google Sheet (or whatever you use — keep it simple):

```
| Name | Vertical | Address | Phone | Visited (date) | Talked to | Outcome | Follow-up date | Status |
```

**Status values:** `cold`, `walked-in`, `interested`, `trial booked`, `first run done`, `repeating`, `dead`.

## Targets for week 1

- 30 walk-ins (A1 lawyers + A3 florists + A4 print shops, all ≤ 1 mi)
- 5 trial runs booked (free first run as opener)
- 2 of those become repeating within 14 days
