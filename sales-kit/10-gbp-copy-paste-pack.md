## Reviews — request flow

After every transaction (mailbox setup, delivery, notary, bundle close):

**SMS template:**
```
Thanks for using NOHO Mailbox! If you have 30 sec, a Google review really helps a small NoHo shop like us:
[YOUR_GBP_REVIEW_SHORT_LINK]

— NOHO Mailbox · STOP to opt out
```

**To get your short review link:**
1. Inside `business.google.com`, click "Get more reviews"
2. Copy the short URL — looks like `g.page/r/XXXXX/review`
3. Paste into the SMS template above

**Review reply rules:**
- Reply to **every** review within 48 hours
- Address the reviewer by name
- For 5-star: thank them, mention something specific from their review
- For 4-star: thank them, ask what would have made it 5
- For 3-star or below: apologize, address the specific issue, offer to make it right via phone
- Never offer a discount in exchange for a review (FTC violation)

## Common mistakes that tank GBP rank

- ❌ Different name spellings across listings ("NoHo Mailbox" vs "NOHO Mailbox")
- ❌ Address with or without "Suite" / different ZIP digits
- ❌ Phone number formatted differently (`(818) 506-7744` vs `818-506-7744`) — pick one and use it everywhere
- ❌ Service area set to "all of LA" — Google deprioritizes
- ❌ Skipping the Q&A — competitors will fill it with their own answers
- ❌ Generic photos (stock images) — only real photos rank
- ❌ Buying reviews (Google detects, suspends)
- ❌ Different business description than your website meta — Google flags
