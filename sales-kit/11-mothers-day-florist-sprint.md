## Why this works

Florists overcommit on Mother's Day weekend every year. They know it. Most don't have a backup plan because every other courier costs $25+ minimum and requires a contract. A walk-in offer of "we're 2 doors down, $5/stop, just guess and I'll hold the team" is unique enough to land.

The ROI on this single sprint is asymmetric — even one florist signing for Mother's Day + Valentine's + Christmas + Bouquet Day weekly recurring gets you to ~$400/month sustainable revenue from a single relationship.
