## Tracking + Optimization

**Conversion events to track in Google Ads**:
1. Form submit on /pricing (phone or web inquiry)
2. Form submit on /business-solutions (LLC bundle inquiry)
3. Form submit on /delivery (delivery quote)
4. Form submit on /for-cmra-operators (SaaS demo)
5. Form submit on /affiliates (affiliate application)

**Optimization rhythm**:
- Week 1-2: Manual CPC, watch for waste keywords (no clicks → pause; clicks but no conv → pause after 3 clicks)
- Week 3-4: Review search-terms report, add negatives
- Week 5+: Enable Target CPA bidding (CPA target: $40 for mailbox, $100 for LLC bundle, $80 for delivery, $250 for SaaS demo)

**Expected unit economics** (rough):
- Mailbox: $25 CPC × 4% conv rate = $625 CPA. Too high. Aim for $1.50 CPC at 4% = $37 CPA → break-even after 2-3 month subscription
- LLC bundle: $7 CPC × 8% conv rate (for LLC-specific kw) = $87 CPA. $2000 sale = healthy
- Delivery: $5 CPC × 6% = $83 CPA. $5-$24 per run → need 5+ runs lifetime to break even (most regulars do this)
- SaaS demo: $7 CPC × 12% to demo, 30% demo→close = $194 CPA. $299-$1499/mo recurring → 1-2 month payback

## Search Intent Pyramid

Top of pyramid (highest intent, lowest volume, highest conversion):
- "north hollywood mailbox rental"
- "$2000 llc bundle"
- "ipostal1 alternative LA"
- "court filing service los angeles"

Middle (medium intent, medium volume):
- "virtual mailbox los angeles"
- "california llc formation"
- "same day delivery los angeles"

Bottom (lower intent, high volume):
- "mailbox"
- "llc"
- "courier"

We bid heavy on top, light on middle, never on bottom. The $/intent ratio is
much better at the top.
