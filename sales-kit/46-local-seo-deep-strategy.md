# Local SEO Deep Strategy

The 90-day local SEO playbook to dominate "[service] near me" searches
in NoHo / Burbank / Studio City / Glendale / wider LA. Goes beyond
the GBP copy-paste pack (doc 10).

**Goal**: rank #1-3 for 50+ local search terms in our service area.
Local SEO compounds harder than national — fewer competitors, higher
intent, faster wins.

---

## How local SEO actually works

Google Maps + local search results show:
1. **Local Pack** (top 3 GBP listings)
2. **Local Finder** (full GBP results)
3. **Organic results** (regular blue links)

Ranking factors (in rough order of importance):

1. **Proximity** — physical distance from searcher
2. **Relevance** — does GBP / website match the search intent?
3. **Prominence** — review count, review velocity, citations,
   backlinks

Proximity is mostly fixed (your location). Relevance + prominence
are what you control.

## Days 30-90: prominence builders

### Reviews push

Continue weekly review acquisition:
- 3-5 new reviews per week (sustainable)
- Reply to every review within 24 hours
- Address negative reviews per doc 42 Section 6

Target by day 90: 60+ reviews, 4.7+ star average.

### Local citation expansion

Beyond the obvious listings:

- LA-specific directories: LA411 (production-industry), LosAngeles.com,
  LACraigsList business listings, LA Tourism (if you serve tourists)
- Industry-specific: BBB, USPS-CMRA listings, Pack Store associations
- Niche local: Patch (NoHo / Burbank / Studio City editions),
  community newsletters

Each new citation = small prominence boost. Diminishing returns
after 30-50 citations; don&apos;t spam.

### Local backlinks

Higher-quality wins than mass citations:

- LA Business Journal (contribute opinion piece on a relevant topic)
- LA Daily News (local business angle)
- San Fernando Valley Sun (local feature)
- Local chambers of commerce (NoHo, Burbank, Studio City, Glendale)
- Local Rotary / Kiwanis / business groups
- LA-area podcasts (be a guest)
- LA-area newsletters (sponsor or guest write)

Aim for 3-5 high-quality local backlinks per quarter. Quality > quantity.

### Geo-targeted blog content

Beyond the existing ZIP-by-ZIP pages:

- "Best [service] in [neighborhood]" pages
- "Walking-distance businesses in NoHo Lankershim corridor" — local
  context articles
- "[Neighborhood] business owner roundup" — interview-based content

Goal: 1-2 new geo-targeted pieces per month.

## Reviews — the core local SEO lever

### Volume targets

- 20 reviews: minimum threshold for credibility
- 50 reviews: common consideration tier
- 100+ reviews: premium tier (most competitors won&apos;t hit this)

### Velocity targets

- 3-5 new reviews/week (sustained)
- Don&apos;t exceed 8/week (Google flags suspicious velocity)

### Star rating

- 4.5+ stars: healthy
- 4.0-4.4: action plan needed
- Below 4.0: crisis (review individual reviews, fix root causes)

### Acquisition tactics

What works:
- Personal email asks ("Hey [Name], if our service has been good
  to you, a Google review takes 30 seconds: [link]")
- In-store signage with QR code
- Receipt mentions ("Thanks for your business — review us on
  Google: [link]")
- Following up immediately after a successful interaction
- Replying to every review (shows we care + signals engagement)

What doesn&apos;t work:
- Mass-emailed review begging
- Incentivizing reviews (against Google ToS)
- Buying fake reviews (detected + penalized)
- Asking unhappy customers to review

### Negative review handling

Reply within 24 hours. Public reply should:
1. Acknowledge their experience
2. Apologize for what fell short (without admitting legal liability)
3. Offer to make it right offline (provide email or phone)
4. Don&apos;t argue facts publicly

Then reach out privately and resolve. If resolution happens, kindly
ask if they&apos;d update the review.

## Geo-keyword content strategy

Beyond ZIP-by-ZIP pages:

### "Best [service] in [LA neighborhood]" pages

Pick 5-10 LA neighborhoods. Write a 600-800 word page each:

- Local context (what makes this neighborhood unique)
- Why our service fits this neighborhood
- Specific use cases for businesses there
- Adjacent zones we serve
- CTA + map embed

Targets: Studio City, Burbank, Sherman Oaks, Glendale, Hollywood,
West Hollywood, Pasadena.

### Local "vs" content

- "NoHo Mailbox vs UPS Store Burbank"
- "NoHo Mailbox vs iPostal1 Studio City"

These rank for "[competitor] [neighborhood]" searches.

### Neighborhood-specific use cases

- "Same-day delivery for solo attorneys at Stanley Mosk"
- "Real address for Studio City florists going wholesale"
- "Burbank realtor going independent — what to set up first"

These rank for very long-tail, very high-intent searches.

## Paid local search

If budget allows ($300-500/mo to start):

### Google Local Service Ads (LSA)
- Pay-per-lead model (no clicks)
- Limited categories — verify if our category qualifies
- High quality leads when we qualify

### Google Ads with location targeting
- Geo-target the LA metro area
- Target keywords from doc 19
- Heavy use of GBP-linked ads (use call extensions, location
  extensions)

### Yelp Ads
- Generally lower ROI than Google
- Worth considering if your competitors are heavy on Yelp

## What this strategy ISN&apos;T

- Not a substitute for great service. Local SEO amplifies what your
  business actually IS.
- Not for non-LA expansion. We&apos;re LA-specific. National brand
  building is a different motion.
- Not quick. 30-day quick wins help, but real local SEO compounds
  over 6-18 months.
